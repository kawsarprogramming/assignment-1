Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh has a young population that is tech-savvy and has increasing internet penetration. The government is promoting e-governance, improving digital infrastructure, and fostering a startup ecosystem.

Sustainability

Bangladesh is investing in sustainable infrastructure to withstand climate change. This includes healthcare and education facilities, as well as physical infrastructure like the Padma Bridge, the Dhaka Metro Rail, and the Payra Deep Sea Port..
